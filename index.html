THE STORY

Created as a surprise gift combining my son's favorite gaming genres - crafting, alchemy, and snake games. What started as a gift to my son to play while I was away for work has spawned an epic cosmic adventure.

This game will always remain 100% free because we believe in sharing the small joys of new discoveries. New elements, combinations, lore fragments, skins, and features are added weekly. We're expanding the cosmos with each update.

How to Play Infinite Snake

🎮 Controls

Desktop: Mouse to steer OR WASD keys

WASD: A/D to turn, W to boost

Mobile: Virtual joystick to steer, Boost button to accelerate

Boost: Click/W (Desktop) or Boost button (Mobile)

Keyboard: P to pause, M to mute/unmute

🎯 Goal

Grow your snake by collecting elements and discovering new combinations! Climb the global leaderboard, unlock new snake skins, build your Discovery Journal, and compete in Daily, Weekly, Monthly, and All-Time rankings.

🔬 Element System

• Collect basic elements: Earth 🌍, Air 💨, Fire 🔥, Water 💧

• Your snake can hold up to 6 elements

• Elements automatically combine when compatible!

• Example: Fire + Water = Steam ☁️

• Over 10,000 elements available today!

• New elements and combinations added regularly!

📖 Discovery Journal

• Build your collection of discovered elements

• Your discoveries persist between sessions

• Track your progress and unlock achievements

• Compare your collection with other players

• Access from the pause menu

✨ Power-ups

🌀 Void Orb: Clears element inventory and converts to points. May produce a random element!

💎 Catalyst Gem: Summons 3 rare undiscovered elements not in the current spawn pool

⚔️ Combat

• Collide head-on with smaller snakes to eliminate them

• Collect their dropped elements for points

• Avoid head-on collisions with larger snakes!

• Use boost strategically to escape or attack

🏆 Game Modes

🏆 Classic Mode: Competitive survival with 3 lives

• Survive as long as possible to achieve high scores

• Game ends permanently on 4th death (permadeath)

• Scores submitted to leaderboard only on permadeath

• Defeat bosses to earn extra lives (up to 3 max)

∞ Infinite Mode: Practice and exploration

• Start with 3 revives, then unlimited respawns

• No score submission to leaderboard

• Perfect for discovering new combinations

• Great for learning boss patterns

👹 Boss Encounters

• Ancient beings known as Deep Old Ones emerge from the void

• Each has unique attack patterns

• Defeat them to unlock exclusive snake skins

• Earn rare elements and special buffs

📜 Lore Unlocks

• Defeat bosses to uncover their backstories

• Reach score milestones to unlock world lore

• Discover rare elements to reveal secrets

• Track your lore discovery progress

• Visit infinitesnake.io/lore for full library

💡 Tips

• Use boost strategically - it drains stamina!

• Experiment with different element combinations

• Stay away from the map borders!

• Check back regularly for new elements

• Plan your element combinations for max points
