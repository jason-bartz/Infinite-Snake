Key Changes Implemented:

  • preyRatioMax: Fixed prey detection to allow aggressive snakes to hunt similar-sized prey
  • bodyAvoidanceMultiplier: Personality-specific body avoidance strength
  • playerAvoidanceRadius: Cautious snakes actively avoid the player
  • Hunting Logic: Fixed formula from lengthRatio < (1 / fleeThreshold) to lengthRatio <= preyRatioMax
