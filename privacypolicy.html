Privacy Policy for Infinite Snake

Last Updated: July 23, 2025

Introduction

Welcome to Infinite Snake ("we," "our," or "us"). We respect your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal information. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard information when you play our browser-based game.

By playing Infinite Snake, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this policy.

Information We Collect

Information You Provide

  • Player Name: When you choose to participate in leaderboards, you provide a player name that will be publicly displayed.

Information Automatically Collected

  • Gameplay Data: Your scores and game statistics associated with your player name for leaderboard functionality.
  • Technical Data: We may collect basic technical information such as browser type and game performance metrics to improve the game experience.

Information Stored Locally

  • Game Progress: Your game settings and progress are stored locally in your browser using localStorage technology. This data remains on your device and is not transmitted to our servers unless you participate in leaderboards.

How We Use Your Information

We use the collected information to:

  • Display your player name and scores on public leaderboards
  • Provide and maintain game functionality
  • Improve game performance and user experience
  • Respond to support requests

Data Sharing and Disclosure

We do not sell, trade, or rent your personal information to third parties. Your player name and associated scores are publicly visible on leaderboards. We may disclose information if required by law or to protect our rights and safety.

Data Retention

  • Leaderboard Data: We retain player name and score data as long as necessary for leaderboard functionality.
  • Local Storage: Game progress stored in your browser remains until you clear your browser data or delete it manually.

Your Rights and Choices

You have the right to:

  • Access: Request information about what data we have about your player name
  • Deletion: Request removal of your player name and scores from our leaderboards
  • Opt-out: Choose not to provide a player name and play without leaderboard participation

To exercise these rights, contact us at support@infinitesnake.io

Children's Privacy

Infinite Snake is intended for general audiences. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe we have collected information from a child under 13, please contact us immediately.

Data Security

We implement appropriate technical measures to protect your information. However, no method of electronic storage is 100% secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

International Data Transfers

Our game is accessible worldwide. By using Infinite Snake, you consent to your information being processed in the country where our servers are located.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page and updating the "Last Updated" date.

Do Not Track

Our game does not currently respond to Do Not Track browser signals.

Third-Party Links

Our game may contain links to third-party websites. We are not responsible for the privacy practices of these external sites.

Contact Us

If you have questions or concerns about this Privacy Policy or our privacy practices, please contact us at:

Email: support@infinitesnake.io

Consent

By playing Infinite Snake and submitting a player name for leaderboards, you consent to our Privacy Policy and agree to its terms.
