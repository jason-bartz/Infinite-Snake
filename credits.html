The Story Behind Infinite Snake

I created Infinite Snake as a surprise for my son. He absolutely loves Infinite Craft and snake.io, so before leaving for a work trip, I wanted to give him something special, a game that combined both of his favorites. What started as a surprise gift has become something we both enjoy playing together.

The game will always be 100% free because I want other families to enjoy this unique experience too. I'm committed to continuing to improve the game and add new features regularly. If you'd like to support development, you can buy me a coffee.

Inspiration

Special thanks to Neal Agarwal for creating Infinite Craft, and to the classic Snake games, snake.io and slither.io that provided the foundation for gameplay.
